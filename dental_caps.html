Dental Caps & Crowns

Restore your smile with our premium dental caps and crowns. Expert care for damaged or weakened teeth.

Types of Dental Caps

Porcelain Crowns

Natural-looking, durable, and perfect for visible teeth. Matches your existing teeth color.

Metal Crowns

Extremely durable and ideal for back teeth. Made from gold alloy or base metal alloys.

Porcelain-Fused-to-Metal

Combines strength and aesthetics. Good option for front or back teeth.

Ceramic Crowns

Metal-free option with excellent aesthetics. Perfect for people with metal allergies.

The Procedure

1

Examination

Thorough dental examination and X-rays to assess tooth condition.

2

Preparation

Tooth reshaping and impression taking for custom crown fabrication.

3

Temporary Crown

Placement of temporary crown while permanent one is being made.

4

Final Fitting

Permanent crown placement and adjustment for perfect fit.

Aftercare Tips

Oral Hygiene

Brush twice daily and floss regularly around the crown.

Diet

Avoid hard foods and sticky candies that could damage the crown.

Check-ups

Regular dental visits to monitor crown condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do dental crowns last?
With proper care, dental crowns typically last between 5 to 15 years.
Is the procedure painful?
Local anesthesia is used during the procedure to minimize discomfort.
How much do dental crowns cost?
Costs vary depending on the type of crown and your specific needs. Contact us for a consultation.
